# BROTO — Briefing Visual 2 · 20 Prompts de Asset

Continuação do [primeiro briefing](broto-briefing-visual.md). Estes 20 são **novos** — não repetem os 15 anteriores. Cobrem o que o site pede agora que cresceu: os **21 conceitos**, mais **personas**, os **15 setores** do catálogo e material de **TikTok/Reels**.

Cada prompt tem ~2.000 caracteres e já traz luz, lente, grading e lista de "evitar" embutidos — é só copiar inteiro.

## A bíblia (a direção de arte de tudo)

- **Paleta:** papel cremoso `#f3ece0`, verde broto `#3e8e4f` / profundo `#285f34`, terracota `#c25d2e`, ouro-sol `#d9971c`, tinta quente `#2b2118`.
- **Mundo:** cerrado brasileiro. O orgânico encontrando o financeiro. Luz natural dourada, grão de filme, materiais reais.
- **Regra de ouro:** gere 3–4 variações e escolha **a menos perfeitinha**. A imperfeição é o que tira o cheiro de IA.
- **Ferramentas:** foto → Midjourney v6 / Flux · vídeo → Sora, Veo 3, Runway Gen-3.

# PARTE 1 — Personas (ampliar o elenco)

## 1 · A manicure / cabeleireira do bairro
**Uso:** seção "pra quem é". **Formato:** 4:5.

```
An honest editorial film portrait of a Brazilian woman in her late twenties who works as a manicurist or hairdresser, photographed inside her own small neighborhood salon during late afternoon. The only light source is a window to camera left, warm and low, wrapping one side of her face and falling off into soft shadow on the other — a natural Rembrandt-style falloff, no fill light, no reflector, no flash. She looks directly into the lens with a calm, self-assured half-smile: composed and a little amused, never the wide commercial grin of stock photography. Her skin is completely un-retouched, with visible pores, freckles, acne scars or fine lines left exactly as they are; natural unstyled hair; simple work clothes with a professional apron that shows real use. Behind her, the honest clutter of a working salon rendered slightly out of focus: bottles with faded labels, a mirror with photographs tucked into the frame, a small plant, an old fan, a hairdryer on a hook, a calendar. Nothing staged or tidied for the camera. Technical: shot on a 50mm prime at f2 on 35mm film stock, shallow depth of field, focus locked on the near eye, fine natural grain, gentle halation blooming off the window, slight lens vignette, tiny chromatic aberration at the frame edges. Color grade strictly warm and muted — cream #f3ece0 walls, terracotta #c25d2e accents, dusty sage green props, warm skin tones; lift the shadows slightly so nothing crushes to pure black. Composition: vertical 4:5, subject positioned slightly off-center with clean breathing room above her head for a caption to be overlaid later in design software. Mood: dignity and quiet competence — a woman who works with her hands, handles cash every single day, and has never once been spoken to honestly about money. Reference the humanism of Magnum photojournalism and Brazilian documentary portraiture. AVOID: studio backdrops, blue or teal tint, neon, beauty retouching, plastic skin, perfect symmetry, corporate wardrobe, glamour lighting, stock-photo enthusiasm, any visible brand logo. Photoreal, high resolution.
```

```
A quiet, dignified editorial portrait of a Brazilian man around twenty-two years old, sitting on the edge of a simple bed in a modest bedroom in early morning. Warm sunlight enters through a thin curtain to camera right, throwing a soft glowing rectangle across the bedsheet and part of his shoulder. His phone lies face down on the mattress beside him — visibly present but deliberately set aside — and he is looking toward the light, thoughtful and steady, caught at the beginning of a decision rather than at the end of a collapse. This is a portrait of resolve and a fresh start. It must NOT read as shame, addiction, despair or rock bottom: no head in hands, no dark oppressive tones, no scattered bottles, no gambling app or betting imagery visible anywhere, no dramatic misery. The warmth of the light does the emotional work. His posture is upright and calm; his expression is serious but open, maybe on the edge of relief. Real un-retouched skin with visible texture, natural unstyled hair, simple everyday clothes — a plain t-shirt, shorts. The room is modest and true: a painted wall with a small mark or two, a single plant on the windowsill catching the light, folded laundry on a chair, a glass of water. Technical: 35mm prime at f2, shallow depth of field with focus on his eyes, natural light only, no artificial fill, fine film grain, soft halation where the sun hits the curtain, gentle contrast with lifted shadows. Color grade warm throughout — cream #f3ece0, terracotta #c25d2e, honey light, sage green from the plant; absolutely no blue, no cyan, no cold morning grade, no desaturated "depression" look. Composition: vertical 4:5 with generous negative space to his side, so the image breathes and text can sit there. Mood: the first calm morning after choosing to stop — steady, hopeful, human, and above all respectful of the person. AVOID: exploitation, pity, staged sadness, casino or betting iconography, phone screens showing anything, neon, cold tones, stock-photo acting. Photoreal, documentary, cinematic, high resolution.
```

# PARTE 2 — Os conceitos como imagem

## 5 · Juros compostos (o tempo trabalhando)
**Uso:** conceito 🌱. **Formato:** 16:9 ou 3:2.

```
A single elegant editorial still-life photograph showing four stages of the same plant growing, arranged left to right on a long raw wooden table lit by one warm window light from camera left. From left: a bare seed resting on soil; a sprout with two small leaves; a young plant with several branches; and finally a small sturdy sapling with many branches and dense foliage. All four sit in identical simple terracotta pots filled with the same dark cerrado soil, so growth is the only variable in the frame. Critically, the visual jump between the third and fourth plant must be clearly larger than the jump between the first and second — the image has to show acceleration, because that is the entire idea of compounding. The light falls off gradually from left to right so the largest plant sits in the richest warm light. Technical: shot on a macro or 50mm lens at f4 so all four plants stay legible and sharp enough to compare, single warm window source with deep soft shadows and no fill, dark cream wall behind, fine 35mm grain, subtle halation, gentle highlight rolloff. Slight asymmetry in leaf placement and a few loose soil crumbs on the table keep it from looking rendered. Color grade warm and painterly — cream #f3ece0 background, terracotta #c25d2e pots, sprout green #3e8e4f and deep green #285f34 foliage, honey #d9971c light; no blue, no cold tones. Composition: horizontal 16:9, the four pots occupying the lower two thirds, with clean negative space above for a headline. The quality reference is Dutch still-life painting crossed with premium magazine food photography — tactile, quiet, and expensive-looking. AVOID: text, numbers, arrows, charts, coins, money, graphs drawn over the image, 3D rendering, CGI plants, plastic-looking leaves, perfect symmetry, studio seamless backdrops, neon. Photoreal, high resolution.
```

## 7 · Dívida cara (o balde furado)
**Uso:** conceito 💳 — o mais importante do site. **Formato:** 4:5 ou vídeo de 6s.

```
A striking conceptual still-life, warm and completely literal. An old galvanized metal bucket, dented and weathered with age, sits on cracked terracotta-colored ground in warm low afternoon sunlight. Someone just out of frame is patiently pouring water into it from a simple watering can — while a clear, steady stream escapes through a hole near the bottom of the bucket, running down the side and pooling briefly before disappearing into the dry cracked earth. The low sun catches the water both as it falls into the bucket and as it leaks out, making the loss beautiful, golden and impossible to miss. Focus must sit on the leak, not on the pouring: the escape is the subject. Technical: shot on a 50mm lens at f2.8, shallow depth of field with the hole and the escaping stream tack sharp, high shutter speed to freeze individual droplets mid-air, warm low backlight from camera right so the water glows amber, natural light only, fine 35mm film grain, slight lens flare accepted. Color grade strictly warm — cream #f3ece0 sky, weathered galvanized grey on the bucket as the only neutral, terracotta #c25d2e cracked soil, honey #d9971c lit water. The water must never read as blue or cold; it should look like liquid light. Composition: vertical 4:5, the bucket slightly off-center and low in frame, with clean space above for a headline. Mood: patient effort being quietly wasted — filling something before fixing it. Calm and rural rather than alarming, because the brand explains rather than scolds. If produced as video: a six-second seamless loop of the pouring and the leaking, with gentle handheld micro-motion. AVOID: text, coins, banknotes, money symbols, credit cards, dollar signs, arrows, cartoon exaggeration, 3D rendering, blue water, studio lighting, neon, any human face. Photoreal, editorial, high resolution.
```
